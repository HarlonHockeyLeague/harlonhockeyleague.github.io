Champions of Excellence: Celebrating the HHL Season 2 Award Winners

Jun 20, 2023 • Harlon Hockey League

In a thrilling culmination of the HHL Season 2, the league's most prestigious awards have been bestowed upon the outstanding individuals who demonstrated exceptional skills, growth, and leadership. The community cast their votes, and the winners have emerged, their names forever etched in HHL history.

Taking center stage is the Most Valuable Player (MVP) award, a testament to the player who has shone the brightest throughout the season. The coveted title and a substantial reward of 15,000 HLP go to none other than Ocelotek1234POL. With an impressive 81.8% of the votes, Ocelotek1234POL's contributions and impact on the game have been truly unmatched. Their exceptional gameplay, strategic prowess, and relentless dedication have set a standard for excellence in the HHL. Westray, who secured the second position with 18.2% of the votes, deserves commendation for their remarkable performance as well.

The accolade for the Most Improved Player (MIP) recognizes the player who has displayed remarkable growth and progress. This season, the deserving recipient is SwiftGolem059. Their relentless pursuit of improvement, evident through their hard work and dedication, has earned them the admiration of the community. In recognition of their outstanding journey, SwiftGolem059 is awarded 7,000 HLP as a testament to their commitment and perseverance. Gertis7, who secured the second position, with 27.3% of the votes, deserves acknowledgment for their impressive improvement as well.

Moving on to the esteemed title of Coach of the Year (COY), it is with great honor that the award is presented to Optimized_. As a mastermind behind their team's success, Optimized_ has showcased exceptional leadership, strategic brilliance, and unwavering support for their players. Their guidance and expertise have propelled their team to new heights, making them a force to be reckoned with. In celebration of their exceptional coaching prowess, Optimized_ will receive 7,000 HLP as a token of appreciation. Kangaroo567, who secured the second position with 30% of the votes, deserves recognition for their remarkable coaching efforts as well.

These outstanding individuals have not only left an indelible mark on the 2nd season but have also inspired future generations of players. Their unwavering commitment, relentless pursuit of excellence, and remarkable achievements embody the spirit of competition and sportsmanship that define the HHL.

As the season concludes and the victors bask in their well-deserved glory, the HHL community eagerly awaits the next chapter, eagerly anticipating the awe-inspiring performances and fierce battles that lie ahead. Congratulations to all the winners and participants for making HHL Season 2 an unforgettable journey of passion, skill, and camaraderie.
