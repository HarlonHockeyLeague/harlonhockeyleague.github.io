Hibbing Gardens: Home of the Acricene Woks

Apr 19, 2023 • Harlon Hockey League

The HHL is pleased to announce the opening of a brand new arena in the Hibbing Gardens area, located just outside of Arcricene State. The new stadium, which will be home to the Acricene Woks, has a large seating capacity and is easily reachable by public transport via metro line M3.

We are thrilled to have this new facility available for our league. This state-of-the-art arena will provide our players and fans with an exciting new venue to enjoy our sport. The Acricene Woks are lucky to call this arena home, and we can't wait for the first games to be played here.

The new arena is the second in the HHL, and its opening signals the league's continued growth and expansion. muffinbarde, the league's CEO, praised the new arena as a valuable addition to the HHL and expressed his excitement for future games to be played there.

"The opening of this new arena is a testament to the hard work and dedication of everyone involved in the HHL," said muffinbarde. "Our players, coaches, and fans will now have access to one of the finest facilities in the league, and we look forward to seeing the exciting games that will be played here."

The HHL is proud to have the new arena as part of its growing network of venues and looks forward to the future of the league.
