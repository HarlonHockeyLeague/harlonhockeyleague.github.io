Opening Weekend of HHL Season 2

Feb 16, 2023 • Royal Eagle News

The first matches of the Harlon Hockey League were held last weekend. The games held included:

Limaru Ligers v. Tryon Miners: 0-6
Harlon Huskies v. Stratham Swingers: 10-3

Currently, the Harlon Huskies are leading this season, with the current leaderboard of:

Harlon Huskies
Total Goals: 10
Matches Won: 1

Tryon Miners
Total Goals: 6
Matches Won: 1

Westfield Hares
Total Goals: 3
Matches Won: 1

Redmont Gliders
Total Goals: 3
Matches Won: 1

Sealand Pirates
Total Goals: 3
Matches Won: 1

For any match where a team arrived but the opposing team did not show up; the team that showed up earned 3 goals and 1 win by default. This is the reason why some teams listed above have goals despite not playing games yet.

A few teams did not show up despite notice, including the Vindex Coyotes and the Shoretrench Sailors.

For up-to-date information about the hockey games and the scores, the HHL website has all the details: https://hhl.harlonserver.net/
