Huskies and Hares Stay Undefeated

Apr 14, 2023 • Harlon Hockey League

  • Huskies continue perfect start with dominant 17-0 win over Gliders in HHL round three
  • Hares thrash Ligers 8-2 in HHL round three
  • Sailors and Miners advance with walkover victories in HHL round three


The Ligers were determined to redeem themselves after their defeat in Round 1 and they did so in style, with a dominant 8-2 victory over the Hares. Westray and Niche each scored twice for the Ligers, while FlyGuy3000 and _xwhitec added one goal each. The Huskies continued their winning streak with a crushing 17-0 victory over the Gliders, with Ocelotek1234POL scoring an impressive 12 goals. The Coyotes secured a walkover win against the Sailors, while the Miners also secured a walkover win against the Pirates. Unfortunately, no team showed up for the Rockets vs. Swingers game.
