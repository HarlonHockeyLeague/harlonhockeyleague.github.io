HHL Season 2 Kicks Off with a Bang!

Feb 12, 2023 • Harlon Hockey League

  • Huskies dominate with 10-3 win over Swingers in HHL season opener
  • Ligers fall 6-0 to Miners in HHL season debut
  • Hares and Sailors advance with walkover victories in HHL round one


The Harlon Hockey League Season 2 kicked off with a bang as the Pirates sailed to a comfortable 3-0 victory against the Rockets, who unfortunately did not show up for the game. The Ligers were outplayed by the Miners, who secured a 6-0 victory with six goals from the golden boot winner. The Hares hopped to an easy victory as the Coyotes did not show up for the game, giving the Hares a 3-0 win by default. The Sailors also secured a walkover win as the Gliders failed to show up. The Huskies displayed their dominance with a 10-3 victory over the Swingers, with Ocelotek123POL, Optimzed_, and KnivesKot scoring a combined 13 goals.
