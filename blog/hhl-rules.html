Harlon Hockey League Rules

February 28, 2024 • Harlon Hockey League

1. TEAM

1.1. Requirements
  • 2+ Players
  • 1 Captain who has to be a player
  • 1 Coach who can be a player
  • The owner of a club can play
1.2. Name
  • Does not need to be Harlon specific
  • Can't copy a team's name but inspiration is allowed
  • You'll need a pretty good logo
1.3. Coaches
  • Coach and Captain can't be the same player
  • Each coach gets a salary per season by the HHL - Starting pay is 2000 Harlonian Pounds or 1500 Redmont Dollars
  • Coaches are the ones holding the team's economy and are therefore the ones who have to pay all team fines
  • Coaches can own a team and still coach
  • Coaches handle all transfers and contracts regarding their team's players. They can get asked to provide these documents by league officials

2. SPONSORING

The sponsoring conditions will get adjusted and published in advance.

3. ECONOMY

3.1. League
  • The CEO of the HHL, muffinbarde, manages the League's budget
  • This budget will be used to pay manager, coach and ref salaries, as well as incentive payments.
3.2. Division 1
  • The responsible of D1, muffinbarde, manages the 1st Division's budget
  • This budget will be used for rewards and hosting events.
3.3. Division 2
  • The responsible of D2, muffinbarde, manages the 2nd Division's budget
  • This budget will be used to host events.
